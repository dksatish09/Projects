# HR Attrition Analysis

## Objective
This dataset focused on employee attrition. The goal is to analyze factors contributing to employee turnover and provide actionable insights for HR and management.


## Dataset
Rows: 1470
Columns: 35  
Source: Kaggle

## Tools Used
Jupter Notebook, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Visualizations
- Department wise attritions
- Gender Wise Attrition


## Business Insights
- Male employees has high attritions due to the job staisfaction, poor relationship with teams and managers ,work life balance and environment satisfaction are the major issues.

## Recommendations
- Train managers and team leaders in soft skills and people management make every month regular one-on-one feedback and recognition.
- provide safety equipmnets and monitor regurlary
- Avoid overloading Tasks and then reduce overtimes.
- Make manpowers are engaging ,so conduct excercise or yoga at the end of the week.
- conduct quarterly health checkups.
