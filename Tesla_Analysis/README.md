# Tesla car Analysis

## Objective
To find the car productions are dropped and find key factors.

## Dataset
Rows: 2640
Columns: 12  
Source: Kaggle

## Tools Used
Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Visualizations
-Month Wise Productions
- Region Wise Delivery
- Year wise Difference


## Business Insights
production are drop due to less of charging stations and delay in the delivery.

## Recommendations
- Focus on months with high estimated deliveries to maximize revenue.
- Implement dynamic pricing based on battery capacity and range enhancements.
-  Ensure uninterrupted electricity supply & backup solar/EV storage support.
- Invest in additional charging stations in areas with growing Tesla adoption but limited charging facilities.
- Collaborate with local governments or energy providers for faster deployment.
