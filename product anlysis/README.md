# Product Analysis

## Objective
To find the Sales of the product are less and find key factors.

## Dataset
Rows: 4566
Columns: 10  
Source: Kaggle

## Tools Used
Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn.

## Visualizations
- category wise total sales
- Top Brands by Orders and Sales


## Business Insights
Products in the fragrence,lingerie and nightwear and jewellery are very less sales.

## Recommendations
- Make combo offers and increase discounts  in the fragrence categories.
- provide a better advertisement for the nightwears.
- To boost sales in the jewellery  make local delears for  exchange offer and provide cashbacks.
- provide faster delivery and make replacement policies.
